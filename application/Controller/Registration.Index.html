How do I sign up?

Registration starts Sunday November 19th 2017. Pick up a flyer and registration form in your parish, fill it out and send it along with your check (made out to Break For Jesus) to address provided in the form. If not announced, ask your parish priest for an application form.


How much?

Cost is $375 per first child, $370 per subsequent child from the same family (brother or sister). Cheques must be dated no later than December 31st 2017.
